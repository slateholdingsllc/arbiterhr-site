Common questions

Before you ask.

When can I actually buy something?+
First products ship in May 2026. The full Phase I library (10 workbooks + 3 bundles) rolls out through the summer. The Phase II live portal opens to founding clients later in 2026.
What does "updated monthly" actually mean?+
On the 1st of every month, every past buyer gets an email with the new edition attached — no re-purchase, no extra fee. You own the latest version forever. The Phase II portal will refresh daily; the workbooks refresh monthly.
What's the difference between the workbooks and the portal?+
Workbooks are one-time purchases ($39 standalone, $129–$249 bundles), live in Excel, work offline, refresh monthly via email. The portal is a subscription at arbiterhr.com/[your-company] — daily-refreshed data, scoped to your states and headcount, with a chatbot trained on the modules you own. Workbooks are the snapshot. The portal is the live feed.
What's "founding-buyer pricing"?+
The first 50 buyers of each workbook get $20 off the launch price and a locked-in subscription rate when the Phase II portal opens. Waitlist members are notified before public launch.
Is this legal advice?+
No. Arbiter HR products are reference materials — citations, summaries, and decision support for HR and payroll professionals. They're not a substitute for legal counsel on specific situations. Every workbook says this on tab 1.
What happens to my email on the waitlist?+
It's used for one launch announcement and, if you become a customer, the monthly edition email. Nothing else. Unsubscribe link on every email.
