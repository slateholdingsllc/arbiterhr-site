Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 26, 2026  ·  Arbiter HR, a brand of Slate Holdings LLC

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. We keep it short and plain because that's how it should be.

1. Who This Applies To

This Privacy Policy applies to all visitors to arbiterhr.com and all purchasers of Arbiter HR products, operated by Slate Holdings LLC ("we," "us," "our"). Contact us anytime at brittany@arbiterhr.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you make a purchase: Transactions are processed through Gumroad. We receive your name, email address, and purchase details. We do not receive or store your payment card information — Gumroad handles all payment processing.

When you subscribe to our email list: We collect your first name and email address through Kit (formerly ConvertKit). Providing this information is entirely voluntary.

When you visit our website: We may collect standard analytics data including pages visited, time on site, browser type, and general location (city/region level). We use this to understand how people find and use the site. We do not use this to identify individuals.

When you contact us: Any information you include in an email to brittany@arbiterhr.com is retained for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To fulfill your purchase and deliver your download
  • To send monthly product updates to customers who have purchased a Reference
  • To send compliance alerts and updates to email subscribers (only if you opted in)
  • To respond to customer service inquiries
  • To understand site usage and improve our products

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Gumroad — purchase processing and digital delivery
  • Kit (ConvertKit) — email list management
  • Vercel — website hosting

Each of these services may collect data in accordance with their own policies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies if you have concerns.

5. Email Communications

If you subscribe to our list, you'll receive our monthly compliance digest and occasional product announcements. Every email includes an unsubscribe link. Click it and you're off the list immediately — no questions, no retention.

Purchase confirmation and product update emails are sent via Gumroad and are considered transactional. You may manage these preferences in your Gumroad account.

6. Data Retention

We retain purchase records for as long as necessary to fulfill our obligations and comply with applicable law. Email subscribers' data is retained until they unsubscribe. Contact us to request deletion of your information at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. Email brittany@arbiterhr.com and we'll respond within 5 business days.

If you are located in California, the EU, or another jurisdiction with specific privacy rights, those rights apply to you in addition to this policy.

8. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies for analytics. We do not use advertising cookies or track you across other websites. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use the site.

9. Children

Our products and website are intended for professionals and are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll notify active email subscribers. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

11. Contact

Privacy questions, data requests, or concerns — email brittany@arbiterhr.com. We're a small team and take this seriously.
